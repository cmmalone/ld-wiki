# Context Engineering

## Overview
An emerging discipline concerned with deliberately designing and managing what goes into an LLM's context window, treating it as a constrained resource rather than a flat space to fill. Practices include structuring context hierarchies, crafting system prompts, using compaction-resistant pinned files, and choosing what to summarize vs. preserve.
