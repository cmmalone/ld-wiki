# GAIA Benchmark

## Overview
GAIA (General AI Assistants) is an agent evaluation benchmark that inverts the usual expert-matching framing: rather than asking whether AI can match specialists, it asks whether AI can reliably do what a competent, resourceful non-expert human can accomplish in roughly five minutes with a browser. Its ~466 questions have short, unambiguous answers but require multi-step reasoning, web navigation, and handling of varied file formats.
