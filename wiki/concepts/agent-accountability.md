# Agent Accountability

## Overview
Agent accountability is the unresolved question of who bears responsibility when an AI agent takes a consequential action — spending money, sending a message, making a booking — that turns out to be incorrect or unwanted. Because agents act autonomously and mistakes may not be caught in time to reverse, accountability cannot be straightforwardly assigned to either the user or the system. This is an active legal, ethical, and design problem.
