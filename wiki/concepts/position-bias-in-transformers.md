# Position Bias in Transformers

## Overview
The tendency of transformer-based language models to assign systematically different levels of attention and influence to tokens based on their position in the input sequence, independent of their semantic content. Early and late tokens tend to be over-weighted relative to middle tokens.

## Across Episodes
- **S2E3**: S2E3 traces position bias from empirical observation (the 2023 lost-in-the-middle paper) to mathematical proof (the 2025 MIT paper by Xin Yi Wo et al.), establishing that it is an inherent structural property of causal transformers, not merely a training artifact. → episodes/S2E3.md
