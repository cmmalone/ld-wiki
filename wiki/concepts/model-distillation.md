# Model Distillation

## Overview
Model distillation is a compression technique in which a smaller 'student' model is trained to replicate the outputs of a larger 'teacher' model. The goal is to produce a model that achieves similar answer quality at lower computational cost. Distillation optimizes for correct outputs but does not explicitly preserve the model's ability to explain or communicate its reasoning.
