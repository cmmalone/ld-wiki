# MAST: Multi-Agent System Failure Taxonomy

## Overview
MAST is a UC Berkeley (2025) taxonomy of 14 distinct failure modes observed in multi-agent AI systems, derived from expert annotation of over 1,600 execution traces across seven popular agent frameworks. The failures are organized into three categories: specification and system design failures, inter-agent misalignment, and task verification and termination failures.
