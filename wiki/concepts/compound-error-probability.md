# Compound Error Probability in Multi-Step Systems

## Overview
In any sequential process where each step has an independent probability of failure, the overall success rate is the product of per-step success rates. This causes end-to-end reliability to degrade exponentially as task length grows, even when individual steps appear highly reliable.
