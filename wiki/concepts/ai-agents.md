# AI Agents

## Overview
Autonomous systems built on language models that operate in a loop of observing their environment, reasoning about what to do, and taking actions — repeatedly, across many steps. Agents differ from simple chatbots in that they accumulate context dynamically and must maintain coherent goals over extended task horizons.
