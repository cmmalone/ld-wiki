# Verifiability

## Overview
Verifiability refers to the degree to which the correctness of a task output can be checked automatically and unambiguously. Code is highly verifiable because it can be executed and its results compared against expected outputs; most other work products — reports, strategic decisions, management judgment — exist on a spectrum toward low or near-zero verifiability.
