# Credit Assignment (Long Horizon)

## Overview
Credit assignment is the problem of determining which decisions or actions in a sequence were responsible for a final outcome. In reinforcement learning and multi-step agent systems, long action sequences make it difficult to trace a failure back to its root cause — a plan flaw, a bad tool call, or a misinterpretation several steps back.
