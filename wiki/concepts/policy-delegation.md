# Policy Delegation

## Overview
Policy delegation is the act of giving an AI agent a standing set of rules or criteria — a 'policy' — and then stepping out of the decision loop entirely. Instead of approving each action, the user specifies intent upfront and trusts the agent to apply judgment repeatedly over time. This is common in always-on agents like email managers and raises deep questions about authorization and oversight.
