# Game of 24 (Benchmark)

## Overview
A combinatorial math puzzle in which a player must combine four given numbers using basic arithmetic operations to produce the value 24. Used as an AI benchmark because it requires genuine search through a space of possible operations rather than pattern matching or plausible next-token prediction.

## Across Episodes
- **S2E5**: The primary benchmark in the Tree of Thoughts paper and the lead example in S2E5. GPT-4 solved 7% with standard prompting, 4% with chain-of-thought, and 74% with Tree of Thoughts — making it a vivid demonstration of the planning capability gap. → episodes/S2E5.md
