# Collaboration Gap

## Overview
The collaboration gap refers to the observed disconnect between an AI model's ability to solve a task independently and its ability to solve that same task when required to coordinate with another agent. High solo performance does not reliably predict high collaborative performance, suggesting collaboration is a distinct and separately learnable capability.
