# Pass@K Metric

## Overview
Pass@K is a benchmark reliability metric that measures whether a system can successfully complete the same task K consecutive times. Unlike a single-run success rate, it surfaces consistency and robustness, capturing the practical requirement that deployed systems must work reliably, not just occasionally.
