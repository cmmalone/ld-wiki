# Error Amplification in Agent Hierarchies

## Overview
In hierarchical multi-agent architectures — where an orchestrator delegates to sub-agents and synthesizes their results — errors made by lower-level agents propagate upward and can corrupt the entire task. The more layers in the hierarchy, the more opportunities for errors to compound before reaching the final output.
